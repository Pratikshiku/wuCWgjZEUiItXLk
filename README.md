## Community Property Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/79d9b3e146484b6a8f22ce617c9be38a/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Feature Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular users, Staff, Administrators.

**Regular User Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Personal Center
4. Change Password
5. Add Owner Information
6. Fee Information, Pending Payment
7. Building Information
8. Repair Information
9. Parking Space Information
10. My Parking Information
11. Complaint Information
12. Announcement Information

**Staff Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Personal Center
4. Building Information Management
5. Repair Information Management
6. Complaint Number Information Management
7. Announcement Information Management
8. Department Information Management

**Administrator Features:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. User Management
4. Staff Management
5. Owner Information Management
6. Fee Information Management
7. Building Information Management
8. Repair Information Management
9. Parking Space Information Management
10. Parking Information Management
11. Complaint Number Management
12. Announcement Information Management
